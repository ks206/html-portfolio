The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies on Disney+

Big Hero 6

Agreeably entertaining and brilliantly animated, Big Hero 6 is briskly-paced, action-packed, and often touching. It was one of my favourite childhood movies.

Avatar

It might be more impressive on a technical level than as a piece of storytelling, but Avatar reaffirms James Cameron's singular gift for imaginative, absorbing filmmaking.

The Sound of Music

Unapologetically sweet and maybe even a little corny, The Sound of Music will win over all but the most cynical filmgoers with its classic songs and irresistible warmth.
